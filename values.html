Core values form the foundations on which a business is built. To be upheld, they must be
implemented across the entire business, known by employees, and referred to throughout the
decision-making process. When used correctly, core values can provide numerous benefits to a company.

What Makes an Effective Core Value

An effective core value is one that stands the test of time. Different to a mission statement, it must come from the heart of the business and reflect the ethos a company is built upon. A value must also be implemented across all company policies and practised in day-to-day business.
